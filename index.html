Interactive Grading

Usage:

  1. Enter a comma-separated, tab-separated, space-separated or newline-separated list of marks. For example, copy a column or a row from an Excel sheet and paste it in the text area.
  2. For fine-grained control on the sliders, click a slider and then use keyboard arrow keys or enter the cut off in the text box next to a grade.
  3. To adjust the axis limits, use the Pan tool or the Box Zoom tool from the toolbar on the right top of the plot. After activating the tool, click and drag on the plot area. To reset the axes, click on the Reset tool.
  4. It is recommended to keep the browser window maximized.
